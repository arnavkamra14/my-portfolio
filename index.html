About Me

Born and raised in the heart of the Silicon Valley, I've always been fascinated by technology and how it powers the world around us. From a young age, I developed a deep passion for understanding technology, which led me to pursue computer science. My initial leadership experiences in Boy Scouts, Student Government, and DECA taught me invaluable lessons about perseverance, teamwork, and community service. These values have profoundly influenced my approach to solving technical challenges.

After attending De Anza College, where I immersed myself in the importance of diversity, equity, and inclusion in tech through my involvement in Women in STEM, I transferred to UC San Diego. Here, I deepened my understanding of software engineering, AI, and data science, developing cutting-edge projects to solve real-world problems. My goal is to leverage artificial intelligence to drive innovation and bridge gaps in society, whether that be in healthcare, public safety, or accessibility. I strive to use my skills not only to push the boundaries of technology but also to ensure that these advancements create a more inclusive and equitable future for all.
